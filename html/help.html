Commands:

  1. Say "Activate TriNayan" to initialize voice assistant.
  2. Say "TriNayan Website Structure" to initialize website structure feature.
  3. Say "TriNayan Speech" to initialize text to speech feature.
  4. Say "TriNayan Customize" to initialize website customizer feature.
  5. Say "TriNayan Change Theme" to toggle between themes.
  6. Say "TriNayan Change to {Theme_Name} theme" to change to specific style.
  7. Say "TriNayan List All Themes" to get all the available themes.
  8. Say "TriNayan Increase Brightness" to increase brightness by 10%.
  9. Say "TriNayan Decrease Brightness" to decrease brightness by 10%.
  10. Say "TriNayan Increase Contrast" to increase contrast by 10%.
  11. Say "TriNayan Decrease Contrast" to decrease contrast by 10%.
  12. Say "TriNayan Increase Size" to increase text size.
  13. Say "TriNayan Decrease Size" to decrease text size.
  14. Say "TriNayan Change to {font_style} style" to change font to specific font.
  15. Say "TriNayan List All Fonts" to get all the available fonts.
  16. Say "TriNayan HelpGuide" to hear all the commands.
